About IEDC SNMIMT

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) at SNMIMT Maliankara is a pioneering initiative supported by the Kerala Startup Mission to foster innovation and entrepreneurship among students.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant innovation ecosystem that nurtures ideas into viable business ventures and develops entrepreneurial mindset among students.

Our Vision

To be a leading hub for student innovation and entrepreneurship in Northern Kerala, producing successful startups and job creators.

Activities & Programs

  • Ideation workshops and hackathons
  • Startup mentorship and incubation
  • Prototype development support
  • Industry connect programs
  • Entrepreneurship awareness camps
  • Funding guidance and investor connects
